Event Planning To-Do List

Major Steps in Planning the Event Required Items for the Event Key Roles and Responsibilities
  1. Initial Planning
    • Determine the event date and time
    • Set event goals and objectives
  2. Venue and Logistics
    • Book the venue
      1. Confirm date availability
      2. Review and sign contract
    • Arrange transportation
  3. Invitations and Communication
    • Create guest list
    • Send out invitations
      1. Design invitation
      2. Send via email or mail
    • Track RSVPs
  4. Event Setup
    • Prepare the schedule and program
    • Arrange for decorations and setup
      1. Choose a theme
      2. Order decorations
    • Organize food and drinks
      1. Select menu
      2. Order catering
  5. Event Execution
    • Coordinate with staff and volunteers
    • Ensure smooth running of the event
  6. Post-Event Activities
    • Conduct clean-up
    • Collect feedback
      1. Send feedback forms
      2. Analyze responses

Event Coordinator
Responsible for overseeing the entire event, ensuring all aspects are covered and tasks are delegated.
Volunteer
Assists with various tasks such as setup, guest management, and clean-up during and after the event.
Vendor Liaison
Handles communication with vendors (caterers, decorators, etc.) and ensures that all agreements are fulfilled.
Logistics Manager
Manages the physical setup of the event, including the arrangement of furniture and equipment.
Marketing and Promotions Specialist
Responsible for promoting the event, handling invitations, and managing social media outreach.
